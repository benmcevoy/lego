Join a wacky car chase with Batman™ and Catwoman™!

Catwoman™ has stolen the milk and a diamond and is escaping in her cool-for-cats Mighty Micros car. Zoom after her in Batman's fast Mighty Micros Batmobile™. Aim his Batarang and stop that thief getting away!

  • Includes 2 minifigures with mini legs: Batman™ and Catwoman™.
  • Batman's Mighty Micros Batmobile™ features turning wheels, Batman™ logo, plus translucent lights and exhaust flame elements.
  • Catwoman's Mighty Micros car features a cat-face design at the front, a cat's tail, plus translucent lights and exhaust flame elements.
  • Batman's Batarang also included.
  • Accessory elements include Catwoman's stolen carton of milk and diamond-style translucent element.
  • Batman's Mighty Micros Batmobile™measures over 1” (3cm) high, 1” (5cm) long and 1” (4cm) wide.
  • Catwoman's Mighty Micros car measures over 1” (5cm) high, 2” (6cm) long and 1” (4cm) wide.
