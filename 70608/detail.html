Master Falls

70608 - The LEGO Ninjago Movie - 2017

312 pieces

Team up with Wu and Kai to take on Jungle Garmadon in this awesome THE LEGO® NINJAGO® MOVIE™ Master Falls set. The jungle bridge features an opening cage and a hidden cave with a skeleton inside holding a secret map. Also includes 3 minifigures with assorted weapons—including Wu’s staff and 4-armed Jungle Garmadon’s 4 swords—to bolster the role-play action.

  • Includes 3 minifigures: Wu, Kai and Jungle Garmadon (with 4 arms and new-for-August-2017 jungle outfit decoration).
  • Jungle bridge features an entrance arch, opening cage, hidden cave with a skeleton holding a secret map, tropical leaf elements and translucent flame-style elements.
  • Weapons include Kai's 2 katanas, Wu's staff and Garmadon's 4 swords.
  • Recreate and role-play exciting THE LEGO® NINJAGO® MOVIE™ scenes.
  • Jungle bridge measures over 6” (16cm) high, 8” (22cm) wide and 5” (14cm) deep.
