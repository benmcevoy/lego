Journey through the Star Wars™ galaxy in the countdown to Christmas!

Take a journey through the galaxy this holiday season with the LEGO® Star Wars™ Advent Calendar! Open each of the 24 doors to reveal a fun Tatooine, Endor, Hoth, Space or Christmas-themed gift. With minifigures, starships, vehicles and more to discover, as well as a great fold-out playmat, Christmas will arrive faster than you can say “May the Force be with you!”

  • Open a window every day to reveal 24 different LEGO® Star Wars themed gifts
  • Features 5 minifigures and 2 holiday-themed Droids
  • Vehicles include Jabba’s Sail Barge, Sandcrawler, Star Destroyer, Millennium Falcon, AT-AT, A-wing Starfighter and a sleigh
  • Also features an Ewok weapon rack, catapult, gun turret, blaster rack and Hoth command post
  • Play out fun LEGO® Star Wars scenes on the fold-out playmat
  • Makes a great festive gift for humans and Sith Lords everywhere!
