High-speed Chase

60138 - City - 2017

294 pieces

Be part of the action with the LEGO® City police as they call in Chase McCain to help stop the crook escaping with the loot in the stolen sports car. This set features a police helicopter with spinning rotors and a lowering winch with hook, police pursuit car, stolen sports car and a barrier. Includes four minifigures.

  • Includes four minifigures: a pilot, police officer, crook and an exclusive for spring 2017 Chase McCain.
  • Features a police helicopter, police pursuit car and a sports car.
  • Police helicopter features spinning rotors, removable cockpit canopy and a lowering winch with hook.
  • Police pursuit car features a rear spoiler.
  • Sports car features space in the back for money bills and gold bars.
  • Use the police pursuit car to try and catch the crook.
  • Call in the police helicopter to assistin the capture.
  • Lower Chase McCain into the stolen car using the helicopter’s winch.
  • Accessory elements include a walkie-talkie, handcuffs, two gold bars, two money bills, plus a buildable barrier.
  • This set offers an age-appropriate build and play experience for ages 5-12.
  • Features an exclusive-for-spring-2017 Chase McCain minifigure.
  • Helicopter with rotor blades measures over 4” (11cm) high, 12” (33cm) long and 10” (26cm) wide.
  • Police pursuit car measures over 1” (4cm) high,4” (11cm) long and 1” (5cm) wide.
  • Sports car measures over 1” (4cm) high, 4” (12cm) long and 1” (5cm) wide.
