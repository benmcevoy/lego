Fire Starter Set

60106 - City - 2016

90 pieces

Save the dockworker and put out the blaze!

Race the fire hovercraft to the pier and put out the fire! Send the diver into the water to save the dockworker and then help the other firefighters put out the flames on the engine. Keep the pier safe and become a LEGO® City firefighting hero!

  • Includes 4 minifigures: 2 male firefighters, a female firefighter and a dockworker.
  • Features a fire hovercraft with turning propeller and accessories, and a pier with engine and fire elements.
  • Send the diver off the hovercraft into the water to rescue the stricken dockworker.
  • Accessory elements include a wrench, fire extinguisher and a life preserver.
  • Fire hovercraft measures over 1” (4cm) high, 3” (10cm) long and 1” (4cm) wide.
  • Pier measures over 3” (8cm) wide, 1” (4cm) deep and 1” (4cm) high.
