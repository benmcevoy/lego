Stop The Joker and save the Batcave!

To the Batcave, Robin! The Joker is using his crazy catapult to smash the Batcave! Can the heroic crime fighters race to the scene in the Batmobile and lock him up in the prison cell before he wins? This Easy to Build set has all you need to develop your child’s building skills. Create a world of iconic super heroes and villains, from the giant archway to the cell for holding bad guys. It also makes an ideal gift! Includes 3 minifigures: Batman™, Robin and The Joker with assorted weapons.

  • Weapons include Batman’s™ Batarang, The Joker's giant mallet, Robin’s staff, and dynamite
  • Load up the catapult and launch the bombs!
  • Capture The Joker and lock him in the prison cell

  • Includes age-appropriate building instructions
