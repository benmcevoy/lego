Head for the coast aboard the awesome 4x4 with Powerboat!

The powerboat championships are underway. Help the LEGO® City powerboat team to get their awesome craft to the harbor race. Jump aboard the high-powered 4x4, start her up and head for the coast. This huge, orange and black race boat with tinted cockpit and tilting high-performance outboard motor is tipped as a top contender—it’s going to be a great day’s boat racing!
