The Riddler Riddle Racer

70903 - The LEGO Batman Movie - 2017

254 pieces

Take on four Super-Villains at once in THE LEGO® BATMAN MOVIE: The Riddler™ Riddle Racer set, featuring the Riddle Racer vehicle with two hidden flip-up spring-loaded shooters. Team up with Batman™ to defeat The Riddler™, Kite Man™, Magpie™ and Calendar Man™. Also includes Kite Man’s detachable glider pack with posable wings, Batman’s Batarang, Magpie’s dynamite elements and other role-play-inspiring weapons and accessory elements. Includes five minifigures.

  • Includes five minifigures: Batman™, The Riddler™, Magpie™, Calendar Man™ and Kite Man™
  • The Riddle Racer features a dual exhaust with two flip-up, spring-loaded shooters, secret front compartment with two hidden guns, an open minifigure cockpit, plus wing mirror and translucent headlight/indicator light-style elements.
  • Press down the dual exhaust to flip up the engine and fire the hidden spring-loaded shooter.
  • Attach Kite Man's glider pack with posable wings to take the action to the sky.
  • Weapons include Batman’s Batarang, and Magpie’s two dynamite elements.
  • Accessory elements include Batman’s cape and new-for-spring-2017 molded utility belt, The Riddler’s top hat and cane, Magpie’s armor, Calendar Man’s calendar cape, and Kite Man's kite and helmet.
  • This set offers an age-appropriate build and play experience for ages 7+.
  • Riddle Racer measures over 1” (3cm) high, 7” (18 cm) long and 2” (7cm) wide.
  • Kite Man's kite measures over 3” (8cm) wide.
