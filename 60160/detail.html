Jungle Mobile Lab

60160 - City - 2017

426 pieces

Discover exciting things in the depths of the LEGO® City jungle with the Jungle Mobile Lab, featuring a truck with opening sides and roof, a waterfall build with a crocodile and temple with a moving leaf that hides a treasure, plus a buildable Venus flytrap plant with closing ‘mouth’ and a kayak. Includes 4 minifigures plus crocodile and spider figures.

  • Includes 4 minifigures: female explorer, male explorer, scientist and a mechanic, plus crocodile and spider figures.
  • Features a Jungle Mobile Lab with opening side and roof and 2-minifigure cab, plus a kayak with paddle.
  • Also includes a waterfall build with a slide function to reveal the crocodile, swinging vine, stairs to a temple with moving leaf to uncover hidden treasure element, plus a buildable Venus flytrap with opening ‘mouth’ and space for a spider figure.
  • Open the sides and top of the Jungle Mobile Lab to access the equipment.
  • Grab the vine to swing across the water and avoid the crocodile.
  • Slide the crocodile through the waterfall to surprise the jungle explorer.
  • Climb the stairs and use the machete to clear away the leaf and find the treasure.
  • Push the top of the Venus flytrap down to trap the spider… or the scientist’s camera!
  • Accessory elements include a machete, bag, wrench, 2 cameras, compass and a diamond.
  • Jungle Mobile Lab measures over 4” (12cm) high, 7” (18cm) long and 2” (6cm) wide.
  • Waterfall measures over 3” (10cm) high, 4” (12cm) wide and 6” (16cm) deep.
  • Venus flytrap with ‘mouth’ closed measures over 1” (4cm) high, 2” (6cm) wide and 1” (4cm) deep.
  • Kayak measures over 4” (11cm) long.
  • Crocodile figure measures over 4” (11cm) long.
