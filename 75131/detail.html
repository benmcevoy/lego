Resistance Trooper Battle Pack

75131 - Star Wars - 2016

112 pieces

Speed into battle against the First Order forces!

The First Order has been detected nearby and must be intercepted. Load the Resistance Troopers onto the speeder, arm the shooter and speed out on a mission to find them!

  • Includes 4 minifigures: 3 Resistance Troopers and a Resistance Officer.
  • Features a speeder with space for 2 minifigures and a rotating stud shooter.
  • Fire the stud shooter to repel the enemy.
  • Includes 4 stud blasters and extra stud shooter ammo.
  • Also includes 3 Resistance Trooper helmets.
  • Makes a great addition to any LEGO® Star Wars collection.
  • Measures over 2” (6cm) high, 3” (10cm) long and 2” (6cm) wide.
