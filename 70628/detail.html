Learn all the skills of Spinjitzu with THE LEGO® NINJAGO® MOVIE™ Lloyd – Spinjitzu Master set, featuring a Spinjitzu spinner with buildable LEGO brick handle, spinner element with minifigure capsule and a rip cord. Learn different minifigure-spinning, rolling and jumping techniques and even tougher skills using the included weapon rack as an obstacle. Compete against your friends and use your environment to create countless fun Spinjitzu challenges. Includes a Lloyd minifigure with 2 shurikens to hold in the spinner.

  • Includes a Lloyd minifigure.
  • Features a Spinjitzu spinner with buildable LEGO® brick handle, spinner element with minifigure capsule and rip cord, weapon rack with a sword, and Lloyd’s 2 shurikens.
  • Learn The Palm Spin, The Orbit, The Zen, The Leapfrog, The Drift, The Knockout and other cool Spinjitzu moves.
  • Create your own spin-tastic challenges and compete against your friends.
  • Go online for even more Spinjitzu tricks and inspiring videos.
  • Combine with other Spinjitzu Masters spinner sets—70633 Kai, 70634 Nya, 70635 Jay, 70636 Zane and 70637 Cole—for ninja vs. ninja skill showdowns.
  • Spinjitzu spinner (without rip cord) measures over 5” (14cm) high, 3” (8cm) wide and 1” (3cm) deep.
  • Weapon rack measures over 1” (4cm) wide and under 1” (2cm) high and 1” (2cm) deep.
