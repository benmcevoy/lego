Olivia's Creative Lab

41307 - Friends - 2017

91 pieces

LEGO® Friends Olivia’s Creative Lab is where Olivia brings her inventions to life! There’s a desk with a drawing board and opening draw, lots of tools, plus a docking station with a place for each member of her robot family and monitor to show the battery power. Includes a mini-doll figure, plus three buildable robot figures.

  • Includes an Olivia mini-doll figure, plus Zobo, Zuzu and Zobito the buildable robot figures.
  • Features a lab desk with a drawing board and opening draw, a storage box plus a docking station with monitor and space for three robots.
  • Accessory elements include a remote control, tools, binoculars, cup and a baby bottle.
  • Get to work with Olivia and Zobo to make two new robot friends.
  • Put each of the robots on the docking station to charge up and play out controlling them with Olivia's remote control.
  • Get a special LEGO® Friends best friend charm with this set to collect and share with your best friend!
  • This set offers an age-appropriate build and play experience for ages 5-12.
  • Lab desk measures over 2” (6cm) high, 2” (7cm) wide and 1” (3cm) deep.
  • Docking station measures over 1” (5cm) high, 2” (6cm) wide and 1” (3cm) deep.
