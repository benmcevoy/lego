Head for the high seas with the 3-in-1 Ocean Explorer!

Explore the ocean depths with this cool set, featuring a ship with a dark-blue, red, white and gray color scheme, large captain’s bridge, navigation lights, radar, antennas, funnel and a yellow robotic submarine. Operate the ship’s boom to lower the sub into the sea, then submerge and explore! When you feel like a change, rebuild to create a cargo plane or a large submarine.

  • Features a ship with a movable crane boom, large captain’s bridge, navigation lights, radar, antennas, funnel and a yellow submarine with movable arms.
  • Boat features an iconic dark-blue, red, white and gray color scheme.
  • Submarine features a yellow, gray and black color scheme.
  • Turn the crane and lower the submarine into the sea!
  • Move the submarine’s robotic arms to collect samples from the seabed.
  • This set offers an age-appropriate building experience for ages 7-12.
  • Includesmore than 210 LEGO® bricks.
  • 3-in-1 model: rebuilds into a cargo plane or a large submarine.
  • Ocean Explorer measures over 3” (10cm) high, 5” (14cm) long and 2” (7cm) wide.
  • Submarine measures over 1” (3cm) long and under 1” (1cm) high and 1” (1cm) wide.
  • Cargo plane measures over 2” (6cm) high, 5” (14cm) long and 5” (14cm) wide.
  • Large submarine measures over 3” (8cm) high, 3” (10cm) long and 2” (6cm) wide.
  • Combine with the 31040, 31041 and 31043 mini-vehicle sets for extended play.
